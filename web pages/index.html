What we do for you?

Laundry Hub is a community based, non profit organiasation that is dedicated in helping YOU and our fellow South Africans with Laundry and making You look like THE MAN/WOMAN in your workplace. We notice that our fellow South Africans work endless hours in low wage paying jobs that they barely have time to do their laundry and they cant afford a washing machine that is quick and efficient making a lot of our South African go to work looking dirty,poor or untidy leading them to have very low moral at their workplace. This is where we step in!. Laundry hub is fully determined in helping you with your laundry with efffiency and assurance for free. Our services are available weekly from Monday to Friday
